Our take

Lupa addresses the challenges of pet care service providers and pet parents in an industry where managing appointments, client engagement, and care provisions can be daunting. With its integrated platform, Lupa streamlines practice management and enhances client interactions, replacing fragmented systems and manual tasks.

Lupa leverages AI integration in pet care, automating tasks, offering personalised insights, and providing proactive support for providers and pet parents. Through features like online booking and AI-driven veterinary advice, Lupa simplifies operations and enhances pet care standards, setting a new industry benchmark.

It's a comprehensive solution to the challenges in pet care management, replacing fragmented systems with its integrated platform. By streamlining practice management and client engagement, Lupa eases burdens for businesses and enhances the pet ownership experience.
